About SolveSpace

A student-created math learning hub built for collaboration, clarity, and confidence.

Our Mission

To make math accessible and fun for all students through peer tutoring, group study sessions, and interactive resources.

Our Core Values

  • Collaboration: Working together to solve problems.
  • Growth: Supporting students to improve and succeed.
  • Innovation: Creative ways to learn and practice math.
  • Confidence: Helping students believe in their abilities.

Why Join SolveSpace?

  • Access to live tutoring sessions and group study events.
  • Track your learning progress easily.
  • Explore lessons, videos, quizzes, and worksheets in one place.
